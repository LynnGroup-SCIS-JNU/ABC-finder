Abstract: ABC transporter proteins are involved in active transport, both in prokaryotes and eukaryotes. These proteins structurally consist of four domains: two nucleotide binding domains (NBD) and two transmembrane domains (TMD). NBD is conserved during the evolution, and consist of five motifs: Walker A, Walker B, switch region which contains a histidine loop, the signature motif and the Q-motif. Various studies suggested the role of ABC transporters in various inherited human diseases, tumor resistance, cystic fibrosis and in clinical drug resistance. To address this, we have developed ABC-finder a Docker based package for for systematic analysis and visualization of topology of ABC Proteins through web browser. ABC-finder integrated HAMMER and TOPCONS existing tools and in-house python scripts for Django app that present inside a Docker Container .ABC-finder takes a variety of input types ranging from raw Fasta files to direct genome search, processes them through a standard pipeline and visualizes on a web browser. ABC-finder, creates interactive genome browser with clickable downstream data analysis using HAMMER and TOPCONS. Overall, ABC-finder, is an extremely convenient, portable and platform independent.
